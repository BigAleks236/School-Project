Walk McHenry

A formal proposal to expand sidewalk infrastructure in McHenry, Illinois to improve pedestrian safety, accessibility, and long-term community outcomes.

Overview Problem Locations Map Cost Impact Conclusion

Project Overview

McHenry currently lacks continuous sidewalk infrastructure in several residential and high-traffic areas. This proposal recommends a phased expansion of sidewalks to improve safety, accessibility, and mobility.

Sidewalks are essential public safety infrastructure that support students, seniors, families, and individuals with disabilities.

Problem Statement

Many streets in McHenry were designed primarily for vehicle traffic, leaving pedestrians without dedicated walking space.

  • Increased risk of pedestrian injury
  • Unsafe walking routes near schools
  • Limited ADA accessibility
  • Conflict between vehicles and pedestrians
